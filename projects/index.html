Free Community Living room

The Community Living Room at Priory Court Community Centre is a warm and welcoming space where people can relax, play, socialise or simply sit by oneself in comfort. This space is run by William Morris Big Local and funded by Waltham Forest council funding.

The following activities, facilities and support will be on offer:

Comfortable seating in a relaxed space. Tea, coffee, cake, biscuits, fruit.

Quiet solo activities: Books, newspapers, jigsaws, word searches etc.

Creative activities: knitting, sewing, drawing, painting.

Social activities such as board games and cards.
